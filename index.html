Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan 1, 2024
Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy, and we use Google Analytics to collect usage data for the purpose of improving our App and providing you with a better experience.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information through Google Analytics when you use our App:

  1. Usage Data: This includes information about your interactions with the App, such as the features you use, the pages you visit, and the actions you take.
  2. Device Information: We collect information about the type of device you use to access our App, including device model and OS version.
  3. Log Information: When you use our App, Google Analytics collects certain information about your usage. This log data may include your locale, app version, app settings, and the date and time of your App use.
How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through Google Analytics for the following purposes:

  1. Analytics: To analyze and understand how users interact with our App, so that we can make improvements and enhance your experience.
  2. Troubleshooting: To diagnose and resolve technical issues and to ensure the App functions properly.
Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We may share the collected data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in improving our App and providing you with better services. These third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect through Google Analytics. However, no data transmission over the internet or data storage system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of any information you provide to us.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our information practices. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
